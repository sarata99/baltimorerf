Navigating Medicare and Medicaid

Useful Link(s): https://www.medicare.gov/ https://www.medicare.gov/basics/get-started-with-medicare https://www.medicaid.gov/medicaid/eligibility/index.html

In Brief:

Medicare: Medicare is a program aimed toward providing health coverage to individuals aged 65 and older. Please note that medicare will not cover ALL expenses, though it does typically cover most costs (to a point - this varies). Generally, covered costs are those that are absolutely necessary ("medically necessary").

Medicaid: Medicaid is the largest provider of health insurance to Americans. in fact, many people are actually guranteed coverage (i.e. "mandatory eligibility groups"). These groups include: low-income families, people receiving SSI, and many others. The link above porivdes specifics about eligibility criteria, further background information, and notable exceptions that are made to help extend coverage.
